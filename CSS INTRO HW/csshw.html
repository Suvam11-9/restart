CRICKET

Cricket is a team game.

Cricket can be divided into three formats.

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams.

The game is most popular in countries like India, England, and Australia.

The ICC (International Cricket Council) governs the sport globally.

Virat Kohli is one of the best batsmen in the world.

Jasprit Bumrah is a top fast bowler.
