About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer born on November 5, 1988, known for his right-handed batting and right-arm medium bowling. He captained India in the 2008 U-19 World Cup-winning squad. Kohli made his international ODI debut in 2008 and his Test debut in 2011. He holds numerous batting records, particularly in the IPL, and is considered one of the greatest batsmen of all time.


Early Career & Under-19 World Cup Kohli was selected for the Delhi U-15 team in 2002 and played for the Delhi U-17 team in 2004. He was the captain of the Indian U-19 team that won the U-19 World Cup in 2008. He made his first-class debut in 2006 and his ODI debut against Sri Lanka in 2008.
